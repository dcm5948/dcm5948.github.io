Fashion Faves

De'Ja's Fashion Faves

A showcase of my current favorites in fashion. Some of these items I own and some I hope to one day own.

Kapital Century Denim No. 7 Westerner Jacket

This is a jacket from one of my favorite brands, Kapital, which is a japanese Americana brand. I don't currently own this, but it's on my list!

Yohji Yamamoto SS2002 Saeko Tsuemura Vegan Leather Jacket

This is another favorite that I don't own and I think I may never own. Unfortunately, you can only buy this from resellers and this jacket goes for about 5k!

Pearled Ivory Lennox bag

I currently own this bag and I'm obsessed. It's made with leather and pony hair. It's currently my go-to bag!

Adidas Samba x Wales Bonner Core Black

I was on the fence about these shoes for so long but currently own them and I love them. The pony hair (can you see a theme?) is very nice paired with my Pearled Ivory bag

Vuja De Satin Bomber

This is another item I hope to own one day. The satin makes this a very beautiful and different bomber jacket to me!

Issey Miyake Ronan Bouroullec Drawing Beanie

This last item is one that I'm happy I own. I love the colors and the versatility of this beanie. I'm a huge fan of most things Issey Miyake.
