F.A.Q.


What time should I arrive?

All of our seating is first come, first served so arriving early will help get you seated closer to the stage. We reserve the right to release your tickets 15 minutes after the scheduled start time if you have not arrived.

Do you seat incomplete parties

Yes, only one person from each party needs to be here in order to be seated and claim a table.

Do you require a minimum spend at the table?

we kindly ask for a $20 minimum spend on drinks per person
